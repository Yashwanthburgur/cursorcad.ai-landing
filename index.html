The Problem: The Analog Bottleneck

For millions of small factories and fabrication shops, the design process is broken.

  • Wasted Hours: Days spent on manual measurement and repetitive CAD work.
  • High Costs: Expensive software licenses (₹3-10 lakhs for scanners) and a scarcity of skilled designers.
  • Costly Errors: Manual inaccuracies lead to material waste and production delays.
  • Slow Growth: Inability to scale or work with clients who demand digital-first workflows.

Our Solution: AI-Powered Automation

We replace the outdated workflow with an integrated hardware and software solution.

  • 10x Faster: Go from physical part to editable CAD file in minutes, not days.
  • 95% Cheaper: Our system costs a fraction of traditional scanners and expert labor.
  • Democratized Skill: Any technician can operate our system, no CAD expertise required.
  • Digital-Ready: Generate clean STEP/IGES files ready for modern manufacturing.

A Simple 3-Step Workflow

We make professional-grade reverse engineering as easy as using a smartphone.

1

Scan with Ease

Connect our affordable LiDAR scanner to your phone. Point and scan any object to capture its precise 3D geometry in real-time.

2

Let AI Do the Work

Upload the scan to our cloud. Our proprietary AI pipeline cleans the data, recognizes features, and auto-generates a clean, solid CAD model.

3

Export & Manufacture

Your editable CAD file (STEP/IGES) appears in your dashboard. Export it directly into SOLIDWORKS, AutoCAD, or any CAM software for production.
